Tournament Process

PRE-QUALIFYING LEGS

The purpose of these legs is for athletes to gain points which will determine their proper seeding for the qualifying legs starting January 2024

QUALIFYING LEGS

Players will compete to win titles and gain ranking points individually and for their team

GRAND FINALE

Maximum of 6 Teams will be invited to compete for the title of Philippine National Mix Team Champion

Of the 6 Teams, 4 will qualify and be invited to participate based on the ranking from the qualifying legs. To ensure national representaion, 2 wild cards will be alloted, 1 for All-Star Visayas Team and 1 for All-star Mindanao Team, providing there are no teams that will qualify from there geographical areas based on the official ranking from the qualifying legs
